> **PERSONNEL**

> [Seyed Yahya Shirazi, Co-Pl,]{.underline} will direct UCSD's
> agentic-integration work: deepening the Open Science Assistant (OSA),

> BEP046/BEP047 maintenance), coordinating the NEMAR Zarr work and

> by agents. He maintains the HED Al-native layer (hed-lsp, hed-mcp,
> HEDit) and is the technical lead for NEMAR, the archive and compute
> gateway used as the project's training/analysis target.
